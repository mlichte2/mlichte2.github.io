Privacy Policy for Comment Allowlist & Blocklist Filter Extension

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Comment Allowlist & Blocklist Filter Extension ("Extension", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our extension.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The Extension collects and stores the following information that you explicitly provide:

  • User Lists: Usernames you add to the allowlist and blocklist
  • Preferences: Your chosen settings for the extension's functionality

Information Collected Automatically

The Extension does not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Collect personal information
  • Monitor website content beyond what is necessary for its functionality
  • Send any of your data to external servers

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  1. Providing Core Functionality: Filtering comments according to your specified preferences on smartmoneytrackerpremium.com
  2. Saving Your Preferences: Storing your settings locally in your browser to maintain your preferred configuration

Data Storage

All data collected by the Extension is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Send your data to remote servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes

Data Security

The Extension uses Chrome's built-in security features to protect your stored preferences. Since all data is stored locally and not transmitted elsewhere, the risk of data breaches is minimal.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over the data used by this Extension:

  • You can view and modify all saved preferences through the extension popup
  • You can delete your data by clearing the extension's storage through your browser settings
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.

Contact Us

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under 13 years of age.

Compliance

This Extension complies with Chrome Web Store's Developer Program Policies and applicable privacy laws.
